Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 1, 2026

("we," "us," or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring your personal data is handled securely and transparently.

Our Role as an Intermediary: We do not hold, store, or manage user funds. BlowPay and BlowMoney function strictly as technology intermediaries. All monetary deposits, fund transfers, and financial transactions are securely processed and held by our licensed, regulated banking and financial partners.

BlowPay and BlowMoney operate on separate domains — blowpay.app and blowmoneypay.com — but are fully managed by a single unified core team and infrastructure, ensuring consistent operations, security, and service delivery across both platforms.

2. Information We Collect

To provide our intermediary billing and financial services, verify your identity, and fulfill regulatory compliance, we collect the following basic personal information from you:

  • Identity Data: Full Name.
  • Contact Data: Email Address and Phone Number.
  • Physical Data: Residential Address.
  • Financial Verification Data (Nigeria): Bank Verification Number (BVN).

Important Note on BVN: We only collect your BVN for mandatory Know Your Customer (KYC) identity verification purposes as required by financial regulations. We never use your BVN to access your bank accounts or view your balances.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the gathered information to:

  • Facilitate bills payment (Airtime, Electricity, Water Board, Internet Plans) via BlowPay.
  • Enable peer-to-peer fund transfers and deposits via BlowMoney.
  • Verify your identity to prevent fraud and comply with global KYC regulations.
  • Fulfill our legal obligations regarding Anti-Money Laundering (AML) and Counter-Terrorism Financing (CTF) tracking.

4. Third-Party Data Sharing & Partners

Because we are a technology intermediary, your information is securely shared with our primary, licensed financial service providers to execute transactions and complete identity verifications.

We share your data with the following onboarded, approved partners:

Flutterwave Paystack Cashonrails

These partners use your data strictly to process payments, cross-reference KYC details, and maintain compliance with legal and central banking frameworks. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third-party advertisers.

5. Data Security & Storage

We employ industry-standard encryption protocols (such as Transport Layer Security/TLS) to safeguard your data during transmission. Because we partner with top-tier financial institutions, your data is handled via secure APIs. While no mobile transmission is 100% secure, we continuously monitor our systems to prevent unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure of your information.

6. Your Data Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request corrections to inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Request the deletion of your account and associated personal data (subject to mandatory regulatory data retention laws for financial transactions).
  • Object to or restrict the processing of your data.

7. Children's Privacy

Our Apps are intended strictly for individuals who are legally capable of engaging in financial transactions (typically 18 years or older). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we discover that a child has provided us with personal data, we will delete it immediately.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the Apps and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document. Continued use of the Apps after updates constitutes acceptance of the changes.
