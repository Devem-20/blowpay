Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about BlowPay.

General Questions

What services does BlowPay offer?
BlowPay offers a range of services, including:
  • Utility bill payments (electricity, water, cable, etc.)
  • Airtime and data top-ups for mobile phones
  • QR code payments at participating merchants
  • Flight bookings for domestic and international travel
How do I get started with BlowPay?
Simply click on the Sign Up button, fill in your details (name, email, phone number), and create an account. Once registered, you can access all our services by logging into your dashboard.
Is BlowPay available in my country?
We currently operate in Nigeria. If you're unsure, contact our support team or check for updates on our platform for coverage information.

Account and Registration

How do I create an account?
To create an account, visit our website or mobile app, click Sign Up, and follow the registration prompts. You'll need to provide your name, email, phone number, and create a password.
What do I do if I forget my password?
Click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page, and follow the instructions to reset your password. A password reset link will be sent to your registered email address.
How can I update my account information?
To update your profile, log in and go to the "Account Settings" section. You can update your personal details, payment methods, and security settings.

Utility Payments

What types of utility bills can I pay through BlowPay?
You can pay for a variety of utilities, including electricity, water, cable TV, internet services, and more. Check the "Utilities" section in your dashboard to see a full list of available services.
Is there a transaction fee for paying utility bills?
Some services may include a small transaction fee, which will be disclosed before you confirm the payment. No hidden fees will be applied.
Can I set up automatic payments for my utility bills?
Yes, you can set up recurring payments for your bills to ensure you never miss a payment. Simply select the auto-payment option during checkout.

Payments and Security

What payment methods do you accept?
We accept multiple payment methods, including bank transfers, credit/debit cards, mobile wallets, and more. Check the "Payments" section for details on available methods.
Is it safe to make payments through BlowPay?
Yes, your payments are protected with the highest level of encryption. We use secure payment gateways to ensure your financial data is safe.
What should I do if my payment fails?
If a payment fails, please double-check your payment details or try using another method. If the issue persists, contact customer support for help.
